Marigold Design

Date created: May 2023

Tools used: Google Sites, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

My role: UI/UX Designer

Marigold Designs is a student-run henna artistry business based in Amherst, MA, dedicated to celebrating and sharing the rich traditions of South Asian culture through intricate, hand-drawn henna designs. Founded in 2022, Marigold Designs specializes in traditional patterns that reflect generations of cultural heritage, offering each client a meaningful and personalized experience. With every design, they aim to foster confidence, connection, and cultural appreciation, one beautiful stroke at a time.

Project Overview:

I designed a polished website for a henna artist to showcase her beautiful portfolio and simplify the appointment booking process. To add a personal touch, I captured custom photos of her work, ensuring the intricate designs were highlighted in an authentic and visually captivating way. The site features several key pages: a Home page introducing the artistâ€™s services, an About Me section detailing her story and creative journey, an Events page listing upcoming workshops and henna events, a Contact page for easy client inquiries, and a user-friendly Booking page for seamless appointment scheduling. Following the initial launch, the website went through a round of user testing, with valuable feedback used to make enhancements. These adjustments helped fine-tune the design, ensuring the site was not only visually appealing but also intuitive and easy to navigate for both the client and her customers.
